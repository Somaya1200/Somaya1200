Somaya Rahimi

MA in Multilingual Communication Technology

University of Geneva

Somaya Rahimi is a master’s student in Multilingual Communication Technology at the University of Geneva.

She holds a bachelor's degree in English translation from Shahin-Shahr University in Iran.
Her interest in learning about other cultures and languages led her to the field of translation.
For her, learning a new language is a chance to be reborn.
After moving to Switzerland, she found this chance by learning French. In 2020, she obtained a diploma in French as a foreign language from the University of Geneva’s School of French Language and Civilization.

With new technologies emerging everywhere, Somaya realized that a major in Multilingual Communication Technology would be the best choice for studying at the Faculty of Translation and Interpreting.
Immigrating to Switzerland was a turning point in Somaya’s life. Starting life in a new place and facing the challenges of immigration inspired her to strengthen her language skills to integrate smoothly into the French-speaking community.
Since 2017, she has worked at a library in Geneva. Her main duties have been to help set up the library’s mediatech, an activity that is preparing her for her next goal - to work in the field of information technology.
